Mobile App

Neon Banking

A futuristic approach to personal finance management, designed for the next generation.

Role

Lead UI/UX Designer

Timeline

3 Months

Tools

Figma, Cinema 4D, After Effects

The Challenge

Traditional banking apps are cluttered and uninspiring. The goal was to create an interface that feels as fluid and exciting as a modern video game, while maintaining trust and clarity.

The Solution

We utilized a dark, high-contrast palette with neon accents to guide user attention. Glassmorphism was used to layer information without overwhelming the screen.

Results

The new design resulted in a 40% increase in daily active users and a significant reduction in support tickets related to navigation.
